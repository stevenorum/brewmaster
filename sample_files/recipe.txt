<h4>Procedure:</h4>
<div itemprop="recipeInstructions">
<p>I cut off any woody parts of the ginger then grate all the ginger I usually buy 2 1/2 pounds to yield 2 pounds grated, Juice and zest all the citrus. Heat in as large a pot that you have available 3 gallons water sugar juice ginger zest and sugar. <br/>
Heat to boil and simmer 45 minutes.<br/>
Cool and transfer to primary pitch yeast<br/>
Add water to just over 5 gallons<br/>
I let everything go in the primary for 1 week<br/>
Then I strain and transfer to secondary<br/>
After 1 - 2 weeks in secondary prime the liquid and bottle<br/>
Let set in the bottles for 2 weeks<br/>
you will have a wonderful ginger citrus drink that is refreshing and well carbonated<br/>
My friends love it (if they like ginger)</p></div>
